The Steves Lab is committed to fostering an inclusive environment for training the next generation of scientists. We value curiousity, proactivity, and a sense of responsibility for our lab, scientific, and broader communities. 

We are currently recruiting graduate students and postdocs! 

Graduate students: If not currently a grad student at UIUC, please apply for admission to the Chemistry PhD program or other academic program according to your interests. Current grad students interested in the group should reach out to Megan about joining. 

Postdocs: Please send your CV and a short (~2 pages) description of your previous research and interest in the group to masteves17@gmail.com.

Undergrads: Please send a CV/resume and short description of your interests to masteves17@gmail.com.
