The Steves Lab is committed to fostering an inclusive environment for training the next generation of scientists. We value curiosity, proactivity, and a sense of responsibility to our lab, scientific, and broader communities.
{: .text-center }

**We are currently recruiting graduate students and postdocs.**
{: .text-center }

---

### Graduate Students

If you are not yet a graduate student at UIUC, please apply for admission to the [Chemistry PhD program](https://chemistry.illinois.edu/admissions/chemistry-graduate-admissions) or another program aligned with your interests. Current UIUC graduate students interested in joining the group are encouraged to reach out to Megan directly.

---

### Postdocs

Please send your CV and a short (~2 pages) description of your previous research and interest in the group to [masteves17@gmail.com](mailto:masteves17@gmail.com).

---

### Undergraduates

Please send a CV or resume and a brief description of your research interests to [masteves17@gmail.com](mailto:masteves17@gmail.com).
